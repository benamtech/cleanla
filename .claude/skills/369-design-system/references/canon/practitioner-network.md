# Practitioner Network — Demoscene + TUI Outreach Map

> **The canon documents the dead and the published. This page maps the living.** Active demoscene members, ANSI artists releasing in 2024–26, modern TUI framework maintainers, demoparty organizers, and academic researchers — with verified contact paths, what each could clarify, and explicit epistemic bounds on what archival research cannot reach. ## Epistemic Bounds — What This Network Can + Cannot Close

### What direct outreach could close (high confidence)

- **Halaster shading method origin** — Sam Roy can clarify
- **Bubble Tea v2 accessibility roadmap** — Will McGugan or Charm team
- **Ghostty mobile + WASM status** — Mitchell Hashimoto
- **Kitty keyboard protocol versioning** — Kovid Goyal
- **Lovebyte judging criteria** — organizers
- **Active 2024–26 PETSCII tooling** — Ko-Ko, Level90
- **iCE founding context** — Frozen Tormentor (if findable)
- **ACiD internal politics 1990–2003** — RaD Man

### What direct outreach could partially close
- **Scene economics** — practitioners know; rarely publish
- **Exact group transition timelines** — preserved in private Discord/Slack
- **Why specific aesthetic conventions formed** — tacit; partial via interviews

### What direct outreach CANNOT close
- **Anonymous artist identities** — many sceners chose pseudonymity by design
- **Pre-1995 group hierarchy** — many founders deceased or unreachable
- **Lost BBS art** — CompuServe + 1980s forum archives permanently fragmented
- **Post-2024 alt.ascii-art diaspora** — Google Groups gateway closed; **discussions migrated to unindexed Discord/Slack**

### Time-sensitive: living-practitioner window
**5–15 year window** for oral histories of 1990s peak generation. ACiD/iCE founders (RaD Man, Frozen Tormentor, etc.) are 50+ years old. **Documentation now or lost.**

## Statement of Outreach Boundaries

This document is a **contact map**, not a record of completed outreach. As of 2026-05-27, the 369 project has NOT systematically contacted these individuals. The map exists so that:

1. Future 369 contributors can act on it
2. Epistemic bounds are explicit ([[knowledge-bounds]] documents what archives can't reach)
3. Time-sensitive opportunities are surfaced (5–15 year living-practitioner window)
4. Phased engagement allows graduated commitment

**The 369 canon as of 2026-05-27 represents what is achievable through systematic archival + database research. Direct practitioner engagement is the next epistemic tier and requires real human contact.**
