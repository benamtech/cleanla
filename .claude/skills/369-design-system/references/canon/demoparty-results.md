## Early Era (1990–1996)

### NAID — North American International Demoparty (1996, Montreal)
- 2-edition event; foundational for North American demoscene
- ANSI/ASCII compo data: not in accessible archive
- Attendees: RaD Man (ACiD), Jason Scott, key artscene figures
- Source: ascii.textfiles.com/archives/1053

### The Party (Denmark, 1991–2002, Aars + Herning)
- **1991** — Inaugural (Dec 26–28); 1,252 attendees; first major multi-platform party
- **1997** — "Batteries not included" edition documented
- ANSI/ASCII compo: specific results unavailable; party focused on Amiga/PC/C64 demos
- Source: demoparty.net/the-party

### Mekka & Symposium (Germany, 1990s)
- Precursor to Breakpoint (ended 2002)
- Multi-platform focus; some ANSI/ASCII activity documented
- Limited archival access to compo results
- Source: roysac.com/blog/2008/01/the-history-of-the-demoscene/

## Digital Archive Era (2011–2019)

### Revision (successor to Breakpoint, Saarbrücken)
- 2011 — first edition, succeeded Breakpoint
- 3,000–4,000 sceners by 2026; largest pure-demoscene party
- ANSI/ASCII compos run annually
- Compo results indexed on Demozoo
- Source: demozoo.org/parties/5512/

### Demosplash (Pittsburgh, USA, Carnegie Mellon)
- **2013** — **Blocktronics ACiD Trip won Freestyle ANSI/ASCII** — 22 artists collaboration, 3,266 lines, longest ANSI scroller ever
- **2018** — ANSI/ASCII compo, specific entries documented
- Source: demosplash.org/compo/asciiansi

### Assembly (Helsinki, Finland)
- Annual July/Aug
- Largest demoparty globally (~10,000 attendees)
- ANSI/ASCII compo categories present 1992 onward
- Specific results from 1992–2010s require direct demozoo.org queries
- Source: assembly.org

### Inercia (Portugal, 2003–present)
- Mid-size regional
- ANSI/ASCII compos documented in recent years (2019+)

### Outline (Netherlands)
- Amiga-focused but ANSI/ASCII categories present

## Acknowledged Gaps

This canon page documents what's *digitally findable* in 2026. Significant gaps remain:

1. **Pre-2005 records fragmented** — many compo PDFs not preserved
2. **NAID** — only 2 editions; results essentially lost
3. **Early Assembly** (1992–2000) — ANSI/ASCII compo PDFs hosted on archived FTP sites; not always indexed
4. **Real-time events** — Revision 2026 results just announced; partial Demozoo entries
5. **Regional Eastern European parties** — Function (Hungary), X / Xenium (Poland) — less indexed

**Total exhaustiveness: ~25%** without direct Demozoo API access, party-organizer outreach, or institutional collaboration. Acknowledged in [[knowledge-bounds]].
