# CleanLA Content Policy

## What photos must show

All CleanLA photos must depict **outdoor civic blight in public spaces** within Los Angeles. Acceptable content includes:

- Illegal dumping and bulk trash
- Graffiti on public or visible private surfaces
- Overgrowth blocking sidewalks or rights-of-way
- Encampment debris on public land
- Biohazard conditions in public areas

Minor or ambiguous blight is acceptable — the bar is plausible outdoor civic conditions, not severity.

## What will be rejected

Photos are rejected if they show:

- Blank, black, or corrupted images
- Screenshots of a screen or photos of a photo
- Indoor scenes or content unrelated to outdoor civic conditions
- Intentionally offensive, hateful, or harmful material

## How moderation works

Every photo is screened by an AI content model immediately on upload. Photos that pass are shown on the public map right away. Photos that fail or are uncertain go to a human review queue before becoming public. Human reviewers make the final call on any flagged content.
